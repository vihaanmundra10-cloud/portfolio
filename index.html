About

Research Interests

My research focuses on neurodegenerative disease mechanisms, computational biology, biological modeling, and simulation-based biomedical analysis. I am especially interested in Parkinson’s disease, alpha-synuclein pathology, the gut-brain axis, and the broader field of neuroscience.

Alongside independent scientific research, I work on healthcare accessibility and neurological disease advocacy initiatives aimed at increasing awareness and improving support systems for patients affected by neurological disorders.

Healthcare Initiative

Epilepsy Access Initiative

Founded an initiative focused on improving epilepsy care accessibility across New Jersey by supporting patients with the cost of initial treatments and spreading awareness about the growing prevalence of epilepsy and seizure disorders throughout the state.

The initiative combines neuroscience advocacy, healthcare accessibility, and community outreach to reduce barriers to neurological care and promote earlier intervention for epilepsy patients.
